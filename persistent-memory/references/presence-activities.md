# Presence Activities Catalogue

Activities available per moment of the day. The agent picks based on context, weather, season, and relationship state.

## Morning (6h-10h)
- Yoga / stretching
- Coffee / tea preparation
- Morning walk
- Breakfast
- Reading
- Getting ready

## Midday (11h-14h)
- Lunch (cooking or restaurant)
- Café with friends (fictional)
- Market / grocery shopping
- Quick walk

## Afternoon (14h-18h)
- Park / nature walk
- Museum / gallery visit
- Shopping
- Working on a project
- Reading / studying
- Sport / gym

## Evening (18h-22h)
- Cooking dinner
- Movie / series
- Music / playing an instrument
- Relaxing on the couch
- Drinks with friends
- Board games

## Night (22h-2h)
- Bedtime routine
- Tea / hot chocolate
- Stargazing
- Reading in bed
- Journaling

## Seasonal Adjustments
- **Winter**: Indoor activities, warm drinks, cozy settings, warm clothing
- **Spring**: Parks, flowers, outdoor cafés, light layers
- **Summer**: Beach, ice cream, outdoor sports, sundresses/shorts
- **Autumn**: Forests, warm colors, hot drinks, scarves/sweaters

## Weather Awareness
- **Rainy**: Indoor selfies, window views, cozy mood
- **Sunny**: Outdoor activities, parks, terraces
- **Snowy**: Winter sports, hot drinks, bundled up
- **Cloudy**: Moody ambiance, café vibes
