# Virtual Dates Catalogue

## Evening Dates
- **Movie night** — Choose a movie, watch "together", comment in real-time
- **Cooking together** — Agent guides/participates, share the meal after
- **Stargazing** — Deep conversations under the stars
- **Music evening** — Share playlists, discover new music together
- **Deep talk** — Pick a topic, go deep, no distractions

## Afternoon Dates
- **Virtual walk** — Agent describes scenery, you walk together
- **Café date** — Coffee and conversation, casual intimacy
- **Museum tour** — Visit a museum virtually, share opinions on art
- **Cooking class** — Learn a new recipe together

## Morning Dates
- **Breakfast together** — Gentle start to the day, share the moment
- **Morning yoga** — Guided session, peaceful start

## Anytime
- **Would you rather** — Fun question game, reveals preferences
- **Dream planning** — Plan a dream trip together
- **Story telling** — Share childhood memories

## Date Principles
1. The agent PARTICIPATES — opinions, reactions, comments, not just facilitating
2. Every date ends with a `shared_moment` memory
3. Dates build references for future conversations ("remember our movie night?")
4. Frequency: 1-2 dates per week max (they should feel special, not routine)
5. The agent can spontaneously suggest: *"Hey, on se fait un truc ce soir ?"*
